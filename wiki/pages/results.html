R E S U L T

Summary

     Our project "Hydrosoilguard" aims to use synthetic biology techniques to produce hydrogen-rich water to replace part of the fertilizer, thereby reducing the use of fertilizer and protecting the soil environment. We have documented in detail the preparation of hydrogen-rich water by four-enzyme cascade catalysis using methanol as a substrate, which you can find on our engineering page. In addition, we have designed 59 different parts, you are welcome to browse our parts page. On our Education and Human Activities page, you can learn how we expand the impact of our projects through a range of educational and practical activities.
